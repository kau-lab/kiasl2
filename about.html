Director's Message

Welcome to the Korea Institute of Air and Space Law (KIASL) at Korea Aerospace University. As we navigate an increasingly complex landscape of aviation and space activities, the role of legal and policy frameworks has never been more critical.

Our institute is committed to conducting rigorous, professional research that addresses the pressing challenges facing the aviation and aerospace industries. We work closely with government agencies, international organizations, and academic institutions to develop informed policy recommendations and advance the field of air and space law.

We invite you to explore our research initiatives, publications, and collaborative programs. Together, we can contribute to the sustainable development of Korea's aerospace sector while upholding the highest standards of legal and ethical practice.

Our Mission

To conduct professional and integrated research in aviation and space law and policy, establishing legal foundations for Korea's aerospace industry development while providing expert policy recommendations to government agencies and international organizations.

Our Vision

To become a leading international research institute in air and space law, contributing to the development of robust legal frameworks that support sustainable growth in aviation and aerospace activities globally.
